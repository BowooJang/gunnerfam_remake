We are Gunner family living in Korea.
We are new gunners,
but we cry and laugh with Arsenal every day.
This homepage is our home,
which we built online, decorated with contents
that show our love for Arsenal.
The whole process of creating all these contents,
such as cheering songs, fanfictures, vlogs,
and diaries, was fun.
The more we support Arsenal,
who plays beautiful football,
the happier our family gets.
Like our family's love,
our love for Arsenal will last forever.
COYG!!
